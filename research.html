Research

Thermal adaptation in tropical mountains

I am focused on analyze the spatial and temporal variation of the thermal physiological tolerances of organisms and their implication upon a climate change scenario and other factors of anthropogenic origin.

Amphibian Conservation

I mainly use amphibians as a study system, although I also work with invertebrates, studying their adaptations to biotic factors (e.g. interactions with predators, competitors, pathogens and organisms at other trophic levels) and abiotic (i.e. temperature).

I integrate efforts along with 'Balsa de los Sapos' Conservation Initiative to start developing applied solutions to facilitate the restoration of wild amphibian populations.

Phenotypic Plasticity

I analyze how organism's phenotype respond to environmental changes.
